F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the most Frequently Asked Questions about Occidental Travel, Applications and Pricing.

  • How much does it cost for a Visa Application?

    We charge Visa Applications based on the country that one wishes to travel to. But on average, it costs Ksh 400,000 for a visa application to most of the countries such as Canada.

  • Is one required to pay the full amount before the visa application process?

    No. Our clients are only needed to pay 25% of the full amount before application. This is a show of commitment on the client's side and is used to cover initial Visa application costs.

  • What if I pay and my visa application is rejected?

    We at occidental Travel have years of experience with regards to visa applications. Therefore we take the client through a series of checks before the application it meets all the the requirements for approval by the commission. Due to our level of professionalism, 99% of our visa applications are always approved.

    However, in the very few, extreme, and unnatural cases where the commission may choose to deny a visa application; Occidental policy on refunds applies as below:

    For a client who had payed the full amount, we offer a refund of 75% back to the client if for any reason the visa application is rejected by the commission.

    On the other hand, for client's who had only payed the initial 25% fee. Please note that this amount is Non-refundable.

  • Are there chances that my visa application may be rejected?

    Statistically, 99% of visa applications done by Occidental are always approved. This is due our many years of exprience in visa applications and our good working reputation with over 50 High Commissions and Embassies.

    We also ensure we take the client through all the required procedures and paper works. Do our pre-application checks to ensure all the pre-application requirements are met and are in full complience with the commission's application standards. This brings the chances of visa rejection to zero. Only in very extreme and unnatural cases do we get a client's application being rejected.

  • In what cases does a visa application get rejected?

    • Past or current Criminal records.
    • False Travel Documents
    • Health Status
    • Status of Your Passport: Damaged or Invalid Passports.
    • Unfavoured Travel History i.e Being on a no-fly-list.
    • Invalid Travel Insurance.

  • Can I pay the full amount once my visa is accepted?

    Yes. Clients are allowed to pay 25% of the total amount before the application process. This acts as the application fee as well as the commitment fee by the client.

    They can clear the remaining 75% once their application has been approved or On collecting their Passport.
